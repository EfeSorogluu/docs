## Welcome to Crafter!

Ready to take your Minecraft server to the future? **Crafter** is a licensed SaaS CMS software that simplifies server management, provides a professional experience for your players, and features completely open-source themes. Bring a new breath of fresh air to your server with its modern design and powerful infrastructure.
